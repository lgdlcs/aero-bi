Courses

Packages tailored to every level, in small groups.

Beginner

4 full days · Monday → Thursday

Off-season courses: 100% available to supervise you

  • Optimised 4-day schedule: 4 FULL days (8 HALF-DAYS) Monday to Thursday, morning and afternoon with possibility of postponement in bad weather and/or continuing in progression at the end of the week.
  • Small groups, 2 min 5 max: individualised progression
    (Aero-Bi reserves the right to cancel the course if only 1 person is registered)
  • Use of tandem educational flight if necessary
  • Price: 650 euros

Programme:

The course lasts 4 FULL days: Monday to Thursday with possibility of postponement in bad weather and/or continuing in progression at the end of the week. During which you will do field practice and basic elementary theory.

Generally 2 days on the training slope interspersed with inflation sessions and tandem educational flight if necessary before accessing the big flight.

You will progressively discover the technique and knowledge needed to fly a paraglider safely.

The goal of this course is to bring you to your first solo flight under your own wing.

What's included:

  • All equipment provided
  • Supervision by 2 instructors
  • Shuttle transport

What to plan for:

  • Lunch (meals can be shared, or bring your own picnic)
  • FFVL insurance licence
  • Accommodation

Progression

À la carte · full or half days

Work towards full autonomy.

  • Each flight includes a briefing and debriefing
  • 35€/flight (school gear) · 30€/flight (your gear)
  • Radio-guided by instructor
  • Discover new flying sites
  • FFVL certification possible

Join us for full or half-day PROGRESSION sessions, to train at your own pace. Goal: reach full autonomy on known school sites in calm conditions. You can join this type of course as soon as you've completed a beginner course, regardless of the number of big flights you've done.

What we offer:

  • Enrich your theoretical knowledge
  • Improve your ground handling and especially your positioning
  • Let you practise as much as needed — how far? Until you feel comfortable without us.
  • Discover new supervised sites, always with guided flights
  • Personal adaptation, individualised exercises

Each new big flight will be preceded by a theoretical briefing "why / when / how", then radio guidance from the instructor (if needed), then a debriefing of course.

As you progress, we can also offer you to validate the theoretical and practical certifications: "initial licence" and "pilot licence".

Pascal 'K-loo' Leclerc

State-certified instructor · Founder of Aero-Bi

Originally from Normandy, K-loo has been flying since the age of 14. Tandem pilot in 1994, federal instructor in 1998, state certification in 2002, speed riding qualified in 2009.

After working at several paragliding schools, he founded Aero-Bi in 2006 to share his passion in the stunning setting of Morzine-Avoriaz.

Today, he offers tandem flights and courses in paragliding, speed riding and mini-wing — still with the same passion.
